About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer and Information Science at the University of Michigan–Dearborn. My research focuses on private edge computing, where home routers and WiFi access points are leveraged as user-controlled compute nodes to complement public edge and cloud infrastructures. I am broadly interested in edge systems, federated learning, and QoS-aware web services.
